French Toast

HOME

Description

French toast is certainly an all time favorite comfort food, and a perfect way to start the day!
Fluffy, and full of protein, smother it in syrup or peanut butter, or load it up with your favorite fresh fruit!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • salt to taste
  • 6 thick slices of bread (brioche is best)
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnomon, and salt together in a shallow bowl until combined and smooth.
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat. Soak each slice of bread in eggy mixture for about 5 to 10 seconds per side. This allows it to absorb the mixture without becoming overly soggy.
  4. Transfer to the hot skillet. Cook until golden brown and slightly crisp, 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Serve hot, add your favorite toppings, and enjoy!
